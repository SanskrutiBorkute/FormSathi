Correspondence Address
पत्रव्यवहार पत्ता
Optional Field 5 of 14
What is this?
This is the address where you currently live and want to receive letters, notices, or documents. It may be different from your permanent (home) address — for example, if you live in a hostel or rented room while studying.
✅ Example
Room 4, Vijay Nagar Hostel,
Amravati Road, Nagpur – 440033,
Maharashtra
💡 Tip
If you live at the same address as your permanent address, you can simply write "Same as above" or leave it blank — both are accepted.
