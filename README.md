# Abstract

Government forms, application forms, and official documents often contain complex language that many users find difficult to understand. People frequently make mistakes while filling forms due to unclear instructions, missing information, or misunderstanding requirements.

FormSathi is an AI-powered form analysis platform designed to simplify form understanding and validation. The system uses Optical Character Recognition (OCR), Artificial Intelligence, and document processing techniques to extract form content, explain fields in simple language, identify missing information, validate entries, and provide intelligent guidance to users.

The platform aims to reduce form-filling errors, improve accessibility, and make official documentation easier for everyone.

# 1. Introduction

Forms play an important role in education, government services, banking, healthcare, and employment processes. However, many users struggle to understand form requirements, resulting in incomplete or incorrect submissions.

Traditional form-filling processes often require manual verification and assistance from experts. This increases processing time and creates inconvenience for users.

FormSathi provides an intelligent digital assistant that helps users understand forms, identify missing fields, and receive AI-generated guidance before submission.

# 2. Literature Review

Several OCR and document-processing systems exist for text extraction and document digitization. Modern AI technologies have also demonstrated significant improvements in document understanding and natural language processing.

Research indicates that combining OCR with Artificial Intelligence can improve document comprehension, automate validation, and enhance user experience.

These findings inspired the development of FormSathi, which integrates OCR, AI-based analysis, and intelligent form guidance into a single platform.

# 3. Methodology

The system works through three major components:

## User

Users can:

* Upload forms and documents
* Extract form content automatically
* Understand form fields in simple language
* Receive validation feedback
* Get AI-generated guidance

## OCR Engine

The OCR module:

* Processes uploaded PDFs and images
* Extracts text from documents
* Identifies form fields
* Converts scanned content into machine-readable text

## AI Analysis Module

The AI engine:

* Explains complex form fields
* Detects missing information
* Validates entered details
* Generates user-friendly guidance
* Provides recommendations for corrections

This creates a seamless and intelligent form analysis experience.

## Working Process

1. User uploads a form or document.
2. OCR engine extracts text from the uploaded file.
3. AI module analyzes extracted content.
4. Form fields are identified and explained.
5. Missing or invalid information is detected.
6. Guidance and recommendations are generated.
7. User reviews suggestions and corrects the form.
8. Final validated form is prepared for submission.

# 6. Limitations

Although the system performs effectively, some limitations remain:

* OCR accuracy depends on image quality.
* Handwritten forms may produce lower accuracy.
* Complex layouts can affect extraction results.
* AI recommendations depend on extracted text quality.
* Internet connection is required for deployment access.
